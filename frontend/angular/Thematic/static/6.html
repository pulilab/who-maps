Monitoring & evaluation: Decisions and activities that enable effective process monitoring and in-depth outcome evaluation, based on project and stakeholder needs

Effective and reliable operations are essential for successful scaling up, and require human inputs and organizational procedures to respond to evolving conditions. As an mHealth product matures and increases its scale, it is critical that the size, structure and competencies of the project team continue to support operations at several levels. This requires a diversity of skill sets to address challenges such as systematic troubleshooting, management of devices, adaptations to the constraints of the technical environment, and other programmatic measures to orient communities and local partners.
