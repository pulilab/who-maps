How my digital health intervention works

Use this framework to present 1) what your digital health intervention does, 2) the health system constraints it addresses, and 3) the essential health interventions it impacts.
You can click on the continuum areas to activate or deactivate a section. Please note that this will affect the Applications part too. Add/remove items Click on the dropdown to select or deselect interventions of the dropdown list. You can select multiple items. Add/remove items Click on the green dot to start forming the balloon, a second click create a balloon of the desired length, balloon can span only contiguous activated columns. Empty balloon are not allowed, once at least one balloon is filled is possible to select the constraints from the rightmost column. Outside of Edit Mode only row with actual content are shown.
